The Second Level

The EFL Champtionship (EPL) is England's second division. This league was founded in 1882. Twenty-four teams participate in the league each season. The Championship's seasons run from August to May with each team playing 46 matches (playing the other 23 teams both home and away). The winner of the league is determined by which team earned the most points at the completion of all 46 matches. Teams are awarded 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, and 0 for a loss. The top two teams at the end of the season are promoted to the Premier League. Teams placed between 3rd and 6th compete in a playoff to earn the third promotion spot. The three teams who earn the least amount of points are relegated(demoted) to EFL League One at the end of the season.
