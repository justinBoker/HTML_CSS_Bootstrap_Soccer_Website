The Top of the Pyramid

The English Premier League (EPL) is England's top division. The EPL was created in 1992, when several of the most lucrative clubs decided to break away from the English Football League in order to pursue greater profits, especially from TV revenue. Twenty teams participate in the league each season. The EPL's seasons run from August to May with each team playing 38 matches (playing the other 19 teams both home and away). The winner of the league is determined by which team earned the most points at the completion of all 38 matches. Teams are awarded 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, and 0 for a loss. The three teams who earn the least amount of points are relegated(demoted) to the EFL Championship at the end of the season.
